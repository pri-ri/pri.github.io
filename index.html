I Make Tech Feel More Human.

I'm Priyadharshini, a Product Manager with a passion for human-centered design and the technical chops to bring it to life. I thrive in that creative space between a user's 'aha!' moment and an engineer's 'how-to', and this site is my canvas to explore it all—from professional deep-dives to personal curiosities.

A Little About Me

Before I was shaping products, I was shipping code. As a full-stack developer, I loved the logic and challenge of building software. But I quickly realised my real passion wasn't just in writing the code, but in architecting the *experience*. I was the developer who was always fascinated by the human story behind every feature.
Making the leap to product management was a natural evolution. It allowed me to combine my firsthand knowledge of building software with my passion for crafting the user's journey—creating products that are not only technically sound, but are a genuine joy for people to use.

My PM Superpowers

  • Full-Spectrum Empathy: I can empathise with the user's needs and the engineer's challenges, making for smoother, faster, and happier development cycles.
  • From Code to Customer: My background allows me to grasp technical constraints instantly, leading to more realistic roadmaps and better-informed decisions.
  • Clear, Concise Communication: Translating complex user stories for engineers, and complex technical trade-offs for stakeholders.

Currently Geeking Out On

  • Beyond the Feature Factory: Setting product goals based on measurable user outcomes, not just a checklist of things to build.
  • AI as a Creative Partner: Exploring how generative AI can be used not just for efficiency, but as a brainstorming tool to spark new and unexpected ideas.
  • The Psychology of Onboarding: How can the first 30 seconds of using a product create a lifelong, happy user?
